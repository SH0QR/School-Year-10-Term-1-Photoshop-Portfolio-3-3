My Design Process
Research
Once the client and I have discussed what they want and how it will work i research how the website might look

I draw in photoshop to give me a rough idea of where things will go and how they will look.
Discuss
The client and I will discuss what features they want in their websited and how i will achieve this.
Develop
Finally I will make the website using HTML and CSS.
